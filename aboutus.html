Margherita is one of the most popular types of Pizza. The toppings contain, basil, fresh mozzarella cheese, a drizzle of olive oil, and fresh tomato sauce. The topping of Margherita pizza may sound simple but they can delight your taste buds. It comes with a thin crust and is cheesy inside.

Raffaele Esposito An often recounted story holds that on June 11, 1889, to honour the queen consort of Italy, Margherita of Savoy, the Neapolitan pizza maker Raffaele Esposito created the "Pizza Margherita", a pizza garnished with tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil, to represent the national colours of Italy as on the Flag of Italy.

Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world for a variety of reasons. It is relatively cheap and easy to make, it is versatile, convenient, and can be customized to suit different tastes. Pizza is also a social food and comfort food that evokes happy memories for many people.
